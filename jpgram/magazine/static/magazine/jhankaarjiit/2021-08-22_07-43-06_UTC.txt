The bond between a sister and a brother is uniquely beautiful. The love, care, silliness, laughter and annoyingness siblings share is pure joy. This bond is signified by a beautiful tradition and festival.
Rakhi is the combination of five meaningful words.

R: rock healthy relationship

A: acceptance all along

K: kindness to the core

H: heartwarming presence

I: idealistic relationship

Jhankaar wishes you all a very happy Rakshabandhan!!✨
