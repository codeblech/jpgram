Choti Diwali marks the beginning of the Diwali celebrations, and many people start decorating their homes on this day to welcome the goddess Lakshmi, who is believed to bring wealth and prosperity. Just like Diwali, Choti Diwali is also associated with the lighting of lamps and candles. The Diyas are lit to symbolize the victory of light over darkness.
