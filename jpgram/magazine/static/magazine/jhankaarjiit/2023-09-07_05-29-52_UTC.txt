"Whatever happened, happened for the good. Whatever is happening, is happening for the good. Whatever will happen, will also happen for the good." - This quote from Lord Krishna in the Bhagavad Gita offer profound wisdom on various aspects of life, spirituality, and self-realization.
Janmashtami is not only a celebration of Lord Krishna's birth but also a reminder of his teachings on love, devotion, and righteousness. It symbolizes the triumph of good over evil and serves as an occasion for spiritual reflection and community gatherings.  On this Janmashtami, may you find strength in Krishna's wisdom and courage in his divine love. Team Jhankaar wishes you a very Happy Janmashtami .
