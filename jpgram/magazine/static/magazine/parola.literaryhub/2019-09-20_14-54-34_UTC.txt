Literature is the mirror of evolution of a society’s cultures and beliefs. For the breadth of knowledge it gives, the moral values it carries and the enjoyment it provides, literature is important.

Laura Ingalls Wilder’s works are considered to have set a benchmark in children’s literature that has persisted timelessly. She’s known for the unique way through which she inspired young minds with essential lessons of life.

Remembering her remarkable contribution to literature, The Joust presents, ‘Humans of Literature’ to all the literary enthusiasts.
