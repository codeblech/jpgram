PART SIX: AFGHAN CIVIL WAR (1992-1996)
(MUJAHEDEEN WARLARDS TURN AGAINST EACH OTHER)

This part of The Afghan Plight series focuses on the second part of the civil war which started in 1992 when the Najibullah communist regime began to crumble and USSR dissolved finally. This civil war covers the rivalries between the different Mujahideen factions which paved the way for Taliban to takeover the Afghan Land.
