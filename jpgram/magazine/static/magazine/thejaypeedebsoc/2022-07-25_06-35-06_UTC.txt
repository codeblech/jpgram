The Jaypee Debsoc presents The Personality of the Week, Droupadi Murmu.

The inspiration serves and enlightens the path of the fearless and empowering leaders. From motivating a tribe to encouraging the whole nation our 15th President, Droupadi Murmu has become the first tribal and the second woman to become the President of India. She was born in Uparbeda village of Mayurbhanj district in Odisha in a Santali tribal family to Biranchi Narayan Tudu. In 2007, she received the Nilkantha Award for the best MLA ( Member of Legislative Assembly) by Odisha Legislative Assembly. Later, she became the first woman Governor of Jharkhand.
