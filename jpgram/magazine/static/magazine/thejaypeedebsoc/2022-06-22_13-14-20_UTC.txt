Pride Month is a time to celebrate the progress that has been made by the LGBTQIA+ community, while also recognizing that there is still much work to be done. 
As we reflect on the progress that has been made, it is important to remember the voices of those who have fought for this equality. Everyone deserves to celebrate who they are, who they love and what they do. Let's celebrate this pride month and the whole LGBTQIA+ community by spreading the love and knowledge. 
The Jaypee debsoc is a space of inclusivity that aims to create a safer and holistic environment for people belonging to every gender, sexuality and different backgrounds. To celebrate the spirit of Pride, The Jaypee Debsoc pays tribute and salutes the indomitable spirit of LGBTQIA+ activists across the globe.
