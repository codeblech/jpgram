The Jaypee Debsoc celebrates the 75th anniversary of The United Nations thereby reaffirming our commitment to the ideals of the UN.
On October 24, 1945, 51 countries came together to create the United Nations. Its purpose was to promote peace and cooperation around the world. Today the United Nations is an organization made up of 193 countries. In every corner of the world, the United Nations is a living testament of hope.
