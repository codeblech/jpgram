Captain Shiva Chauhan of the Indian Army's Fire and Fury Corps is the first woman officer to be operationally deployed at the world's highest battlefield in Siachen

The empowerment and autonomy of women and the improvement of their position in army is crucial for their development in the indian armed forces and is essential for the achievement of sustainable development,  so The Jaypee Debsoc salutes the bravery and Valor of Capt.Chauhan to break stereotypes and becoming an example for young girls.
