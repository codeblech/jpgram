The world of Electronics is too competitive to survive without the touch of "Automation". Automation, can vastly influence the speed, efficiency and reduce human effort for a specific task done in the field of Electronics.
Keeping the same in mind, Microcontrollers based Systems and Robotics Hub(Ucr) introduces its annual Automation based event 'Robo-en-rio'. The line following bot will have to find it's way to the end through a series of different paths and not all of them may lead to the end.

Date : 23 Feb, 2019
Time : 9 am onwards
Venue : JBS Atrium
