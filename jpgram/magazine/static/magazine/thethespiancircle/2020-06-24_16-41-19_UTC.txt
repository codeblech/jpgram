Shadan Ali is one of the most talented actor and the most versatile actor of the thespian circle. He has shown great variations in hos roles from Lord Ram to King Akbar to a small insect. He picks up the most difficult roles to play which no one else can play and has always done justice to them. He is  a good mentor and he does not leave you behind until you perfectly get into the character. He is such a generous and a kind hearted person that he cannot see anyone sad. He is a joyful and a fun loving person and he creates an enlivening atmosphere whenever he is around. He is a funny person and we  rarely see him tensed.  We are very lucky that we could work with him and bidding him farewell would be very tough for us.
