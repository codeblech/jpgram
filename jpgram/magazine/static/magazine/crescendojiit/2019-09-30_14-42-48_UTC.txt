रूपं देहि जयं देहि यशो देहि द्विषो जहि ॥
Navratri celebrates victory of Maa Durga over evil Mahishasura or victory of Lord Rama over demon king Ravana. 
This Navratri, let's pledge to overcome our inner demons and become the conqueror of our own lives.
