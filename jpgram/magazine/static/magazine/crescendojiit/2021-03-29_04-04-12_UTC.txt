Holi, a festival of  joy and colors, a festival to forgive and mend broken relationships and to celebrate all the different faces, the life has for us to offer. It is said that the colors are the elements that give beauty to our vision, they are the “tanmatra”  that shape our emotions and provide us with the indescribable pleasures of life. 
So, on this auspicious occasion of the festival of colors, we at Crescendo wishes you and your family a very Happy Holi. May the canvas of your life gets painted with the brightest colors of happiness.
