New York City is known for its contemporary skyscrapers, charming architecture and  scrumptious food and NYC Central & Co. does not fall far from it. This cosy cafe in sector 37, Noida, is famous among youngsters for its delicacies and amazing ambience. It offers an expansive range of burgers, pizzas, pastas, fries, shakes and many more palatable dishes. NYC Central & Co. is one stop shop for all the foodies.
