Nation celebrates Engineer's Day on September 15 to commemorate the birth anniversary of the greatest Indian Engineer Bharat Ratna Mokshagundam Visvesvaraya. 
The well being of the world largely depends on the work of an engineer.Engineering is not only study of 45 subjects but it is moral studies of intellectual life.Their hardships, struggle, and journey have a different place in the world of science.

CICE wishes you Happy Engineers Day.
