विक्लवो वीर्यहीनो यः स दैवमनुवर्तते।
वीराः संभावितात्मानो न दैवं पर्युपासते॥

'Dussehra' marks the finish of perhaps the greatest celebration, Navratri'. This day denotes triumph of Lord Rama , an avatar of Vishnu, over ten headed Ravana and also Maa Durga's victory over the shape shifting demon king Mahishasura. A time for celebration, a time for good triumphing over evil, a time when the world witnesses the power of good. 
Let us keep the same "true" spirit.

On this promising and devout event, CICE JIIT prays to Lord Rama and Maa Durga to continue to ease up your way of accomplishment and bring flourishing, well-being, and abundance to your homes! Cheerful Dussehra
